Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 June 2025  |  App: Culina  |  Developer: Thomas Borain

1. What we collect

  • Camera images — captured temporarily when you tap the shutter. Images are sent to Google Gemini AI for ingredient detection and are not stored by us or by Google beyond the duration of the analysis.
  • Display name (optional) — stored locally on your device and, if you choose to enter one, also stored in our Supabase database so it persists across reinstalls.
  • Meal history & scanned ingredients — stored entirely on your device using AsyncStorage. We never upload this data to any server.
  • Anonymous device identifier (UUID) — a randomly generated ID created on first launch, used only to authenticate your session with our API. It contains no personal information.

2. What we do not collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, payment details, advertising identifiers, or any analytics data. We have no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

3. Subscriptions & payments

All payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see, handle, or store any payment or billing information.

4. Third-party services

  • Google Gemini AI — processes camera images and generates recipes. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare Workers — acts as an API proxy between the app and Gemini. No request bodies are logged or stored. Subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase — stores your optional display name. Subject to Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple — manages your subscription and payment. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Data retention

Meal history and ingredients exist only on your device and are deleted when you use "Clear all app data" in Settings, or uninstall the app. Your display name in Supabase can be cleared by contacting us. Camera images are never retained.

6. Children's privacy

Culina is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Your rights

You may request deletion of any server-side data (display name) by emailing us. Most data lives only on your device and is fully under your control.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
